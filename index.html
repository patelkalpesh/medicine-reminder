✨ About This App ▼ tap to expand

A complete medicine management app — built with ❤️

🏠 Today — Schedule with Take/Snooze, next dose widget
💊 Medicines — Add/Edit/Pause, photo crop, SOS emergency
📸 Photo — Camera upload with crop tool
⏰ Reminders — 30/15/5 min + sound alert
⏰ Snooze — 10/30 min with countdown
📈 Adherence — Weekly % score
📅 Calendar — Monthly green/yellow/red view
📝 Notes — Record symptoms per dose
❤️ Health — BP/Heart rate log with history
📅 Appointments — Doctor visits with reminders
📦 Stock — Strips, balance, days left, timeline
💰 Costs — Price per strip, future bills
🛒 Order Planner — What to buy for 15/30/60 days
⚠️ Reorder Alerts — WhatsApp/Email/Notify when low
🔁 Course Duration — Day X/Y progress
🏥 Doctors — Contacts, tap-to-call
💬 Caretaker Alerts — Auto WhatsApp if missed
🖨️ Print Card — Fridge summary
📊 Doctor Report — PDF with adherence + notes
📋 History — Export WhatsApp/Email/PDF/Excel
🔄 Backup — JSON export + WhatsApp share
☁️ Sync — Google Drive across devices
🔐 PIN Lock — Secure login
🌙 Dark Mode — Easy on eyes
🌐 Languages — English, Hindi, Gujarati
📲 Install — PWA, works offline
🆕 Auto Updates — Blue banner on new version
📧 Monthly Report — Auto email on 1st of month
